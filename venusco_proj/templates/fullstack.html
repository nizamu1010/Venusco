On the other hand, Flask is a lightweight and flexible micro-framework that gives you more control over your application's structure. It's perfect for small to medium-sized projects and allows you to add only the features you need. With Flask, you'll learn how to create routes, handle requests, and render templates to build dynamic web pages.
